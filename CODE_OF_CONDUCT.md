# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, socioeconomic status, or technical ability.

We pledge that our community will be:

- **Inclusive**: We welcome people from all backgrounds
- **Respectful**: We treat each other with dignity and courtesy
- **Collaborative**: We work together constructively
- **Safe**: We maintain a harassment-free environment
- **Professional**: We focus on the work and ideas, not personal attacks

## Expected Behavior

All community members are expected to:

- Be respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Give and receive constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Use inclusive language
- Respect others' privacy
- Help create a welcoming environment

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments related to gender, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, race, or religion
- Unwanted sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Sustained disruption of conversations or events
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Violent threats or language
- Discrimination based on any protected characteristic
- Other conduct considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Report privately**: Email conduct@example.com with details
2. **Include context**: Describe the incident and any relevant details
3. **Remain respectful**: We take all reports seriously and respectfully
4. **Confidentiality**: We will keep your identity confidential if requested

## Consequences

Violations of this code may result in:

- Private warning from community leaders
- Temporary suspension from community spaces
- Permanent removal from the community
- Escalation to appropriate authorities if necessary

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All community spaces (GitHub, Discussions, Issues, etc.)
- Official community events and conferences
- Any official representation of the community
